What is Multi-AI Command Center?

The WPA Multi-AI Command Center is a public experimental tool for comparing AI-assisted outputs, strategic prompts, institutional reasoning workflows and communication variants within the World Protocol Academy ecosystem.

It is not a military or intelligence operations platform. It is a strategic AI comparison, prompt-testing and reasoning evaluation environment for educational and research purposes.
