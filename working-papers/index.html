This package contains the public-deposit versions of four WPA Working Papers by Sande Smiljanov, Founder & Director of the World Protocol Academy. Plan B in Protocol — Contingency Procedures in Diplomatic Practice

Pages: 65 · Status: Author-Reviewed Final Release
Scope: Contingency planning, ceremonial equivalence, silent transition, pre-authorisation, and crisis-protocol management.
Executive Summary / SOP quick guide:
  • Plan B is the codified second-order protocol framework that protects ceremonial continuity.
  • It is activated by weather, time, security, health, logistics or institutional absence triggers.
  • The public must perceive continuity, not improvisation or collapse.
  • Minimum SOP: trigger matrix, pre-authorised substitutions, alternative routes/venues and communication discipline.

Reading Note · WPA 003 ↔ WPA WORKING PAPER · No. 004

WPA 003 and WPA 004 are companion volumes but distinct papers. WPA 003 is the authoritative standalone Trump–China case study (81 pages). WPA WORKING PAPER · No. 004 is the comparative Trump vs Putin paper (89 pages) that uses the WPA 003 case only as a Trump-side baseline; its analytical centre of gravity is the comparison with Putin's 25 visits, the civilisational products chapter, the micro-protocol signs chapter, and the 360° comparative methodology.

Recommended reading order: WPA 003 → WPA WORKING PAPER · No. 004.
