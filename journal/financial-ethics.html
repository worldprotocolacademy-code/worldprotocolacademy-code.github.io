Финансиска етика

Финансиски firewall, no pay-to-publish, no paid ranking, transparent revenue streams and sponsor disclaimer.

Основни принципи

  • Никакво плаќање за acceptance.
  • Никакво плаќање за побрз review.
  • Никакво платено рангирање.
  • Никакви скриени fees.
  • Никаква guaranteed publication.

Editorial–financial firewall

Editorial Office и Financial Office се одделни функции. Editors и reviewers не знаат payment/waiver статус.

Технички firewall

_stripPaymentFromReviewerView() ги отстранува payment_category, waiver_status, publication_fee, waiver_decision, supporter_status и sponsor_link од reviewer payload.

Дозволени revenue streams

StreamОпис
Production feesSymbolic post-acceptance fees with waivers
Author Support DeskIndependent services
Supporter CircleVoluntary support
Issue sponsorsSigned agreement with independence disclaimer
WorkshopsTraining revenue
GrantsInstitutional grants with independence

Што revenue може да финансира

  • Copy editing.
  • Layout.
  • Metadata.
  • Hosting.
  • Archive infrastructure.
  • Reviewer honoraria.
  • ISSN/DOI infrastructure.

Што revenue НЕ може да финансира

  • Acceptance на ракопис.
  • Fast-track review.
  • Citation manipulation.
  • Editorial Board seat.
  • Political or commercial promotion.

Sponsor disclaimer

Sponsor has no influence over editorial decisions, peer review, acceptance, rejection, article ranking, authors, reviewers or journal content.

Internal financial audit

Annual audit: revenue, expenses, waiver distribution, reviewer honoraria and firewall integrity.

Editorial Transparency Report

Првиот извештај е планиран за December 2026.

Whistleblower протекција

Financial Ethics concerns може да се испратат доверливо на worldprotocolacademy@gmail.com.

Refunds

Tax и invoicing

Fees и donations се фактурираат преку WPA и се запазуваат во accounting records.
