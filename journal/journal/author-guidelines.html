Author Guidelines

Author Guidelines

Everything you need to prepare and submit a manuscript to WPA Journal.

Aims and scope

WPA Journal publishes scholarly and professional work in protocol, diplomacy, public communication, security studies, communicology, cultural diplomacy, institutional communication and protocol scenarios.

Accepted article types

  • Research article
  • Protocol case study
  • Diplomatic analysis
  • Security and communication analysis
  • Review essay
  • Working paper
  • Commentary / professional note

Language policy

WPA Journal accepts manuscripts in Macedonian and English. Titles, abstracts and keywords are encouraged in both languages where possible.

Manuscript format and structure

Submit an editable document. A typical structure includes title, author and affiliation, abstract, keywords, main text with clear sections, conclusion, and references.

Abstract and keywords

Abstract: approximately 150–250 words. Keywords: 4–8 terms describing the core topics of the article.

Citation and reference style

Use a consistent, recognised academic citation style throughout the manuscript. Every reference must be verifiable; do not include unverified or fabricated bibliographic data.

ORCID

Authors are encouraged to provide an ORCID identifier to support unambiguous attribution.

AI-use disclosure

AI tools may assist with language, structure or research support. Authors remain fully responsible for the accuracy of all content. Any meaningful use of AI must be disclosed.

Ethics and conflicts of interest

Authors must follow research and publication ethics and disclose any conflicts of interest. Fabricated citations and false attribution are strictly prohibited.

Originality and plagiarism

Submissions must be original and not under simultaneous review elsewhere. Plagiarism in any form is not accepted.

Editorial screening and evaluation

All submissions undergo editorial screening for scope, quality, citation integrity and ethics.

The review model is being activated gradually and will be clearly stated for each issue or article.

Copyright and license

Authors retain copyright. The default license is Creative Commons BY-NC-ND 4.0 unless otherwise agreed.

How to submit

During the preparation phase, manuscripts are submitted by email to worldprotocolacademy@gmail.com. See the Submit Your Paper page for the required information.

WPA Journal is an independent institutional publication platform. External indexing, ISSN, DOI and other bibliographic statuses are listed only when officially obtained.
