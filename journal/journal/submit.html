Submit Your Paper

Editorial intake for WPA Journal. Submissions are handled by email during the preparation phase.

Editorial intake, not automatic publication

This page explains how to send a manuscript to WPA Journal. Sending a paper does not guarantee acceptance or publication. Every submission goes through editorial screening for scope, quality and integrity.

The form is in preparation. Until the automated submission system is activated, papers are submitted by email to worldprotocolacademy@gmail.com.

Scope of accepted submissions

Protocol, diplomacy, public communication, security studies, communicology, cultural diplomacy, institutional communication and protocol scenarios.

What to include in your submission email

Please structure your email so the editorial office can process it efficiently. Include the following information:

  • Full name
  • Email address
  • ORCID (optional)
  • Affiliation and country
  • Article title and alternative title
  • Abstract and keywords
  • Article type and language (Macedonian or English)
  • Manuscript file (attached)

Required declarations

Each submission must include three short statements from the author:

  • Ethics declaration — the work follows research and publication ethics.
  • Originality declaration — the work is original and not under review elsewhere.
  • AI-use declaration — any use of AI tools is disclosed; the author remains responsible for accuracy.

Copyright and license

Authors retain copyright. WPA Journal publishes under a Creative Commons BY-NC-ND 4.0 license unless otherwise agreed. By submitting, the author consents to editorial communication.

Send your manuscript

When your email is ready, send it to the editorial office. Responses are handled according to editorial availability.
