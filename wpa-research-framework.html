Research architecture

WPA Research Framework

WPA Research Framework ја поставува академската дисциплина на World Protocol Academy: секој јавен труд треба да има автор, датум, abstract, keywords, јасна методологија, references, стабилен URL и PDF верзија кога е можно.

Целта е WPA да биде препознатлива како сериозна истражувачка платформа во областа на протоколот и дипломатско-институционалната практика.

Истражувачки серии

WPA Working Papers

Брзи, академски структурирани трудови за нови теми и работни методологии.

WPA Protocol Studies Review

Периодична публикација за протокол, дипломатија, јавна комуникација и безбедност.

WPA Policy Briefs

Кратки применливи документи за институции, јавни тела и професионалци.

WPA Case Studies

Анализа на церемонии, дипломатски настани, јавни кризи и протоколарни грешки.

Research architecture

WPA Research Framework

The WPA Research Framework defines the academic discipline of World Protocol Academy: each public paper should include an author, date, abstract, keywords, clear methodology, references, a stable URL and a PDF version when possible.

The goal is to make WPA recognizable as a serious research platform in protocol and diplomatic-institutional practice.

Research series

WPA Working Papers

Fast, academically structured papers for new topics and working methodologies.

WPA Protocol Studies Review

A periodic publication on protocol, diplomacy, public communication and security.

WPA Policy Briefs

Short applied documents for institutions, public bodies and professionals.

WPA Case Studies

Analysis of ceremonies, diplomatic events, public crises and protocol failures.

Minimum standard for a WPA paper

ElementRequired content
TitleA clear academic or professional title.
AuthorName, institutional affiliation and contact when applicable.
Abstract150–250 words.
Keywords5–8 keywords.
MethodologyA short description of method, sources and limitations.
ReferencesStructured bibliography and stable links.
MetadataCanonical URL, description, citation format and PDF when possible.
Next step: every new WPA paper should follow the same structure so it can later support scholarly indexing, DOI strategy and international visibility.
