Institutional Positioning

The World Protocol Academy (WPA) provides premium, source-disciplined protocol advisory, executive training, and institutional capacity-building services. Founded by Assoc. Prof. Dr. Sande Smiljanov, WPA translates 26 years of academic, professional, and operational protocol experience into actionable frameworks for ministries, embassies, international organizations, and private-sector diplomatic teams.

Our services are grounded in applied protocol research, including 9 public-source Zenodo DOI working papers and 25 academic publications. We do not sell generic PDFs; we deliver customized institutional engagements, strategic briefings, and protocol audits.
