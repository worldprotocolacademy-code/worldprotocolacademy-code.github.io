Abstract

This WPA Protocol Note (WPA-PN-001, Version 1.4) offers a source-disciplined analytical overview of the protocol, ceremonial and diplomatic-procedural dimensions of the 52nd G7 Leaders' Summit held in Évian-les-Bains, French Republic, 15–17 June 2026. It examines the protocol logic of venue selection, digital security innovations, Swiss arrival coordination, arrival protocol architecture, round-table communication geometry, family photo protocol semiotics, informal summit diplomacy, participant composition and protocol precedence, the Trump–Brigitte Macron ceremonial greeting, the Merz–Trump personalised gift, and bilateral address styles. The Note clearly separates verified facts, visual observations and WPA analytical assessments, and remains open to correction.
