Методологија · Methodology

How this feed works

A scheduled GitHub Actions workflow fetches public RSS/Atom feeds server-side, deduplicates by link, and produces a cautious institutional synthesis for each item. This page reads only the pre-generated JSON files — it never fetches external feeds from your browser and uses no CORS proxy.

Public sources only. This feed aggregates publicly published RSS/Atom items. It does not track individuals, does not access private platforms (LinkedIn, Facebook, TikTok, Instagram), and does not collect classified or private data.

Verification pending. Most source feeds are marked reported_unverified — their URLs are publicly known but not independently confirmed by WPA. Source verification pending. URL restoration does not equal source verification.

Not an evaluation. The synthesis is descriptive institutional context, not an accreditation, official recognition, final ranking, legal or security-operational claim. Master List REV2: 160 records · 159 external records · 155 distinct external institutions · 8 groups (A–D, G–I, R).

Feedspot bridge. Sources you follow on Feedspot can be imported via OPML export (group "F") or an RSS Combiner URL. Feedspot-imported feeds are user sources, clearly separated from the canonical REV2 institutions, and do not change the REV2 taxonomy. Private platforms (LinkedIn, Facebook, TikTok, Instagram) are never imported.
