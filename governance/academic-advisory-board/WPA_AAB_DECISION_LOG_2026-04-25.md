# WPA Academic Advisory Board — Decision Log

**World Protocol Academy**  
**Institute for Protocol, Diplomacy, Public Communication & Security Studies**  
**Date:** 25 April 2026  
**Classification:** WPA-INTERNAL

## Decision 1 — Board status

The WPA Academic Advisory Board is advisory only.

## Decision 2 — Executive authority

The Director remains the executive authority of the Academy.

## Decision 3 — No false recognition claim

The Board does not create accreditation, university status, formal recognition, licensure, or peer-review status.

## Decision 4 — Recommended composition

Target composition is 5–7 members, including academic, practitioner, public communication, ethics/privacy/research integrity, and international perspectives.

## Decision 5 — Relationship to governance documents

The Board will advise on:
- WPA Question Bank Governance Rule
- WPA Research Methodology
- Student Desk Privacy Review Checklist
- future certification integrity
- major publications and benchmark methodologies

## Decision 6 — Recruitment principle

Quality over speed. Substantive engagement is required. No ceremonial name-lending.

## Decision 7 — Public language

Allowed framing:
- Academic Advisory Board
- advisory function
- internal governance discipline
- specialized institute
- growing international platform

Avoid:
- accredited
- university status
- formally recognized
- peer-reviewed by the Board
- world-class/world-leading based only on internal claims
