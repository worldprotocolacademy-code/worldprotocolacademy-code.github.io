**World Protocol Academy**

**Date:** 25 April 2026  
**Classification:** WPA-INTERNAL

## Decision 1 — Methodology status

WPA Research Methodology v0.1 is accepted as an internal working methodology draft.

## Decision 2 — No public overclaiming

The methodology is not a public accreditation claim, marketing claim, or statement of external compliance.

## Decision 3 — AI-use rule

AI tools may assist drafting, editing, translation, summarization, comparison, validation, and formatting, but AI-generated content is not a source and AI-generated citations must be verified.

## Decision 4 — Founder-source rule

Founder-authored works are important WPA sources, but they do not have automatic privileged authority. External scholarship must be engaged where relevant.

## Decision 5 — Peer review language

WPA will not use the term “peer-reviewed” unless external peer review has actually occurred.

## Decision 6 — Certification claims

WPA certifications must not be described as equivalent to academic degrees, formal accreditation, licensure, or internationally recognized credentials unless such recognition is formally obtained and documented.
