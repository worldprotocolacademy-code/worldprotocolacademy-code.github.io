## Core principles

1. WPA research must be evidence-based and methodologically transparent.
2. AI tools are workflow instruments, not citable sources.
3. Founder-authored works are important WPA sources, but do not have automatic privileged status over external scholarship.
4. Claims must be verified.
5. No false accreditation, peer review, or certification claims.
6. Macedonian is an original institutional voice, not merely a translation of English.
