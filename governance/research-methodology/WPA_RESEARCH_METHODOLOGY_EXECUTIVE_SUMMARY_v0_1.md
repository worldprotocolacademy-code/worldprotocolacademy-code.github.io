# WPA Research Methodology v0.1 — Executive Summary

**World Protocol Academy**  
**Institute for Protocol, Diplomacy, Public Communication & Security Studies**  
**Document:** WPA-METH-001  
**Version:** 0.1 — Initial Internal Draft  
**Classification:** WPA-INTERNAL

## Purpose

WPA Research Methodology v0.1 establishes the internal research discipline of World Protocol Academy. It defines what WPA research is, what it is not, how sources are used, how claims are verified, how case studies and interviews are handled, and how AI-assisted workflows are disclosed.

## Scope

The methodology applies to research and research-adjacent outputs in:

- protocol
- diplomacy
- public communication
- security studies
- communication skills

It applies to books, working papers, policy briefs, case studies, protocol analyses, benchmark methodologies, curricula, reports, and WPA-published proceedings.

## Core principles

1. Research must begin with a defined question.
2. Method must be documented.
3. Claims must be supported by verifiable evidence.
4. Limitations and uncertainty must be acknowledged.
5. AI tools may assist the workflow but are not sources.
6. Internal review is not the same as external peer review.
7. WPA does not claim accreditation, peer review, or recognition it has not earned.
8. Founder-authored works are cited under the same standards as other secondary sources.
9. Macedonian institutional language must be original and high-quality, not a weak translation of English.
10. Corrections must be issued honestly when errors are found.

## Important institutional framing

This methodology is an internal working standard. It is not a public marketing claim and not proof of formal accreditation.

## Relationship to existing WPA work

Older WPA outputs may not fully conform to this methodology. New outputs should follow it. Existing outputs should be reviewed against it when they are revised, reissued, or cited as authority.

## Next governance step

The Academic Advisory Board should review this methodology and recommend refinements before it becomes a mature Version 1.0.
