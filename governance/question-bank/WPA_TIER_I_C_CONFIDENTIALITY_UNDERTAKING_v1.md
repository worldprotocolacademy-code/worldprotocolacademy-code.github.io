## 2. Purpose

The person signing this undertaking may receive access to WPA internal or certification-related assessment materials. Such access is granted only for authorized work and only within the scope approved by the Director.

## 3. Protected material

Protected material includes, but is not limited to:

- practice answer keys
- full explanations
- source references
- item review files
- draft questions
- item statistics
- certification pool questions
- certification answer keys
- certification explanations
- certification examination forms
- candidate response data
- any file that permits reconstruction of correct answers

## 4. Classification tiers covered

This undertaking applies especially to:

- **Tier I — Internal**
- **Tier C — Certification**

## 5. Obligations

I agree to:

1. Access protected material only for authorized WPA work.
2. Not copy, export, forward, upload, publish, or disclose protected material except as explicitly authorized.
3. Not store protected material in personal email, public cloud storage, public messaging applications, or unencrypted devices.
4. Not share passwords, tokens, access links, pre-signed URLs, or credentials.
5. Report any suspected exposure, loss, unauthorized access, or accidental sharing immediately to the Director or Question Bank Custodian.
6. Destroy or return working copies when the authorized task ends.
7. Maintain confidentiality after my WPA role or assignment ends.

## 6. Certification material

I understand that certification examination material is the Academy’s most restricted assessment category. I may not use, disclose, preview, copy, or discuss certification items outside the specific authorized task for which access is granted.

## 7. No personal use

I may not use protected material for personal teaching, consulting, publication, commercial activity, training products, or external work unless separately authorized in writing by the Director.

## 8. Breach

I understand that breach of this undertaking may result in immediate access revocation, termination of assignment, removal from WPA-related work, and any lawful remedial action available to the Academy.
