## Mandate

The Question Bank Custodian is operationally responsible for day-to-day compliance with the WPA Question Bank Governance Rule.

## Duties

The Custodian shall:

1. Maintain the master inventory of question bank files.
2. Classify files by tier: P / S / I / C.
3. Verify that public storage contains only Tier P material.
4. Maintain access grant records.
5. Maintain naming and versioning records.
6. Track migration from legacy `protocol-kb` to the new bucket architecture.
7. Receive and document suspected exposure reports.
8. Prepare Material Incident Reports when needed.
9. Produce quarterly governance reports to the Director.
10. Coordinate annual and ad hoc audits.

## Authority

The Custodian may recommend access grants, revocations, file movement, and classification changes. Final authority remains with the Director unless delegated in writing.

## Limits

The Custodian may not:

1. Authorize Tier C access independently.
2. Move material from a restricted tier to a less restricted tier without Director approval.
3. Publish answer keys or restricted material.
4. Reclassify Practice / Knowledge Bank material as certification material without the formal process required by the Governance Rule.

## Reporting

The Custodian reports to the Director and provides quarterly updates covering bucket status, file inventory changes, access grants and revocations, migration progress, incidents, open risks, and recommendations.
