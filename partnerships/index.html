Partnership Doctrine

WPA does not collect logos. It builds structured value relationships.

+3 Strategic Infrastructure Layers

The automation and governance layers that make WPA scalable.

These three are not ordinary partners. They are strategic infrastructure allies that help WPA operate as a world-class automated academy.

+1 Strategic Layer

Proactive Claude Agent

The front-office orchestration layer of WPA. Claude should handle entry, intake, routing, reminders, scheduling, follow-up and conversion logic.

  • Greets and qualifies prospective learners or institutions
  • Routes users to Free, Pro, Academic, Certificate or Institutional pathways
  • Schedules calls, tests, reminders and follow-up steps
  • Supports retention, upsell and progression to higher levels
+2 Strategic Layer

Harvey AI

The legal, rights, compliance and institutional review layer of WPA. Harvey should not be the main teacher; it should be the governance shield.

  • Reviews contracts, partnership documents and policy wording
  • Checks certificate conditions, rights-sensitive workflows and compliance language
  • Supports takedown, attribution, permissions and institutional documentation
  • Provides document-heavy review for serious external and legal-facing processes
+3 Strategic Layer

Verification, Payment and Credential Stack

The operational infrastructure layer for subscriptions, invoices, digital credentials, serial verification and recurring value delivery.

  • Handles subscriptions, payments and membership access
  • Supports certificate serial logic and future digital verification
  • Enables renewals, recurring plans and scaled self-serve revenue
  • Turns WPA from a contact-only academy into a true operational platform

Automation Flow

How the WPA ecosystem should operate in practice.

A unique world-class WPA must not rely on manual chaos. It must run through disciplined layers of intake, learning, review, certification and follow-up.

01

Intake and Routing

Proactive Claude receives the enquiry, asks qualifying questions, identifies the correct pathway and routes the user to Free, Pro, Academic, Certificate or Institutional entry.

02

Scheduling and Preparation

Claude schedules calls, tests, reminders, onboarding steps and pre-assessment instructions with structured follow-up logic.

03

Learning and Assessment

WPAWS and Virtual Sande support lessons, revision, question-bank work, scenario preparation and structured evaluation.

04

Governance and Review

Harvey reviews formal document conditions, compliance wording, rights-sensitive issues, certificate governance and partnership-facing documentation.

05

Certification and Verification

The credential stack handles payment records, serial logic, future digital verification and certificate issuance.

06

Human Authority and Premium Sign-Off

Founder review and human expert sign-off remain essential for high-value, advanced, trainer-level and institution-sensitive cases.
