# WPA Journal Watch Policy

## Status

WPA Journal Watch is an editorial intelligence layer for the WPA Journal.

It is **not** an automatic article generator.  
It is **not** an automatic publication engine.  
It is **not** a replacement for submission, peer review, ethics review or editorial decision.

## What it does

WPA Journal Watch uses public RSS/Atom feed outputs from WPA Watch and maps detected items into:

- topic candidates
- possible article types
- research angles
- editorial queue records
- manual verification status

## What it must never do

- automatically publish a scientific article;
- claim an item is verified without human review;
- bypass paywalls;
- scrape social media platforms;
- invent citations, DOI, ISSN, reviewers or editorial decisions;
- imply Scopus, Web of Science, DOAJ, Crossref or ISSN indexing before formal confirmation.

## Editorial pipeline

Detected item  
→ candidate topic  
→ editorial screening  
→ author invitation or open call  
→ submission  
→ ethics / COI check  
→ peer review where required  
→ editorial decision  
→ production  
→ publication

## AI transparency

AI may support summarization, translation, classification or topic mapping.

AI cannot be listed as author, reviewer or editor.

All AI-assisted text must be reviewed by a human editor before publication.

## Security-sensitive topics

Security, defence diplomacy, crisis communication and state-protocol topics require additional human review before being converted into calls, briefs or articles.
