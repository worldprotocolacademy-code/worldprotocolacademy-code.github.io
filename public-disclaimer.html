Public Disclaimer

On the nature and limits of WPA indexes, benchmarks and institutional comparisons.

World Protocol Academy publishes indexes, benchmark materials and institutional comparisons in the field of protocol, diplomacy, public communication and security. This disclaimer explains what they are — and what they are not.

Important notes

  • WPA is not a state accreditation institution.
  • WPA indexes and benchmark materials are analytical and educational tools.
  • They are not legal judgments, professional licences or official rankings.
  • Data is based on publicly available sources, methodology and human review.
  • Institutions may request corrections.
If you believe a WPA index or benchmark entry about your institution is inaccurate, you may request a correction. See the Correction Request page.
