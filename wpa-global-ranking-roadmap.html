2026–2029

WPA Global Recognition & Ranking Strategy

This roadmap positions WPA as a measurable global institution in the niche of protocol, diplomacy, public communication, security and institutional presence.

Shanghai/ARWU and similar lists are university rankings. WPA will build its own measurable niche position and gain academic weight through partnerships.

Phase 1 · 0–3 months

Institutional identity

Clear use of the name: World Protocol Academy — Institute for Protocol, Diplomacy, Public Communication & Security Studies.

Research page

Publish the research framework, working paper standard and bibliographic discipline.

Metadata hygiene

Every public paper should include title, description, canonical, author, date and citation format.

Phase 2 · 3–12 months

Working Paper Series

Publish regular WPA papers, policy briefs and case studies.

Advisory Board

Build an international advisory/editorial circle of experts.

Partnerships

Partnerships with universities, institutes, public schools, diplomatic centers and professional organizations.

Phase 3 · 12–36 months

Benchmark launch

Pilot the WPA Global Protocol & Diplomacy Benchmark.

Annual Report

Annual report on the state of protocol and diplomatic culture.

Scholarly visibility

Prepare DOI strategy, scholarly metadata and future indexing.

Success metric: WPA should become a recognizable source, methodology and professional reference in its field before targeting formal university rankings.
