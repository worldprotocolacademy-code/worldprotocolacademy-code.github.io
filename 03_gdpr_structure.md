# WPA GDPR Structure – Compliance Checklist

## 1. GDPR Checklist

| Article | Application to WPA | Control | Deadline |
|---|---|---|---|
| Art. 5 | Processing principles | Data minimisation, retention policy | 30 days |
| Art. 6 | Legal basis | Consent, contract, legitimate interest map | 30 days |
| Art. 13 | Information to user | Privacy notice | 30 days |
| Art. 15 | Right of access | User data export | 60 days |
| Art. 17 | Right to erasure | Delete/anonymise workflow | 60 days |
| Art. 25 | Privacy by design | Default minimal data | 60 days |
| Art. 32 | Security | Encryption, 2FA, logs, pen test every 6 months | 90 days |
| Art. 35 | DPIA | Virtual Sande + Student Desk DPIA | 60 days |

- Controller identity
- Data collected
- Purpose of processing
- Legal basis
- Retention periods

- AI processing
- Cookies
- Security measures
- Contact for privacy
