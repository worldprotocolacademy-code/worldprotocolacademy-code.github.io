WPA Trust Doctrine

  • No fake certificates: every certificate must be verifiable by QR, SHA-256 hash and digital signature.
  • No AI hallucinations: no verified source means no final answer.
  • No unsafe administration: all admin actions are logged and governed by role-based access control.
  • No privacy shortcuts: DPIA, GDPR workflows and data minimisation are mandatory.
  • No unverified institutional claims: public security claims must be supported by documentation and review.
WPA security materials are working governance documents and will evolve through internal review, external audit and certification preparation.
