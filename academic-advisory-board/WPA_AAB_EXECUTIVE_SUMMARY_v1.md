# WPA Academic Advisory Board — Executive Summary

**World Protocol Academy**  
**Institute for Protocol, Diplomacy, Public Communication & Security Studies**  
**Document:** WPA-GOV-AAB-001  
**Version:** 1.0 — Final Draft for Adoption  
**Classification:** WPA-INTERNAL

## Purpose

The WPA Academic Advisory Board is an internal advisory body that strengthens the Academy's research discipline, ethics, privacy practice, publication quality, certification integrity, and institutional realism.

## Advisory-only status

The Board advises. The Director decides.

The Board does not:
- manage WPA operations
- approve budgets or contracts
- appoint or dismiss staff
- represent WPA externally unless authorized
- grant accreditation or recognition
- create university status
- substitute for external peer review

## Recommended composition

The Board should have 5–7 members, including:

- senior academic in diplomacy, protocol, public communication, international relations, or security studies
- senior protocol or diplomatic practitioner
- public communication / PR expert
- ethics, privacy, data protection, or research integrity specialist
- at least one international member outside North Macedonia
- optional certification or assessment methodology specialist

## Key duties

The Board advises on:
1. WPA Research Methodology
2. Question Bank Governance
3. Student Desk privacy and AI-use discipline
4. major publications and benchmark methodologies
5. certification integrity
6. avoidance of overclaiming
7. material incidents
8. annual advisory summary reporting

## Honest framing

The Board strengthens internal discipline. It does not create accreditation, peer review status, university recognition, or formal external standing.
