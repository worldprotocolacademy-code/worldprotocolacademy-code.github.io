# WPA Academic Advisory Board — Implementation Checklist

**World Protocol Academy**  
**Institute for Protocol, Diplomacy, Public Communication & Security Studies**  
**Classification:** WPA-INTERNAL

## Phase 1 — Adopt Terms of Reference

- [ ] Director reviews WPA-GOV-AAB-001 v1.0.
- [ ] Confirm advisory-only status.
- [ ] Confirm no accreditation or recognition claim is implied.
- [ ] Sign or approve the Adoption Note.
- [ ] Store internal copy in governance archive.
- [ ] Do not add to sitemap.

## Phase 2 — Define target composition

- [ ] Choose target size: 5, 6, or 7 members.
- [ ] Identify required profile 1: senior academic.
- [ ] Identify required profile 2: protocol/diplomatic practitioner.
- [ ] Identify required profile 3: public communication / PR expert.
- [ ] Identify required profile 4: ethics/privacy/research integrity specialist.
- [ ] Identify at least one international member.
- [ ] Decide whether certification/assessment expert is needed immediately or later.

## Phase 3 — Prepare recruitment materials

- [ ] Prepare one-page WPA institutional summary.
- [ ] Prepare Terms of Reference summary.
- [ ] Prepare Research Methodology executive summary.
- [ ] Prepare Question Bank Governance executive summary.
- [ ] Prepare Privacy Review summary.
- [ ] Prepare draft appointment letter.
- [ ] Prepare confidentiality undertaking.
- [ ] Prepare conflict-of-interest declaration.

## Phase 4 — Approach candidates

- [ ] Contact candidates individually.
- [ ] Explain WPA honestly: specialized institute, growing platform, not state-accredited university.
- [ ] Share advisory-only role.
- [ ] Explain time commitment.
- [ ] Explain confidentiality obligations.
- [ ] Confirm willingness for substantive engagement, not ceremonial name-lending.

## Phase 5 — Appointment

- [ ] Receive candidate acceptance.
- [ ] Collect signed appointment letter.
- [ ] Collect confidentiality undertaking.
- [ ] Collect conflict-of-interest declaration.
- [ ] Record term start and end date.
- [ ] Add to internal Board register.
- [ ] Schedule induction meeting.

## Phase 6 — First Board meeting

- [ ] Circulate agenda.
- [ ] Circulate governance documents.
- [ ] Confirm Chair or meeting facilitator.
- [ ] Review annual priorities.
- [ ] Review WPA methodology.
- [ ] Review question bank governance status.
- [ ] Review privacy/student desk status.
- [ ] Record minutes and action items.

## Do not do

- [ ] Do not market the Board as accreditation.
- [ ] Do not call Board review “peer review.”
- [ ] Do not imply members endorse all WPA activities.
- [ ] Do not publish member names without consent.
- [ ] Do not upload signed member documents to public GitHub.
