World Protocol Academy како специјализиран институт

World Protocol Academy — Institute for Protocol, Diplomacy, Public Communication & Security Studies е специјализирана академска и професионална платформа за развој на знаење, обуки, истражување и мерливи стандарди во областа на протоколот, дипломатијата, јавната комуникација, безбедноста и институционалниот настап.

WPA не се претставува како универзитетски степенодавец. WPA се позиционира како независен институт и академија за професионална извонредност, истражувачки трудови, применета методологија и сертификациски програми.

Institutional formula

World Protocol Academy as a specialized institute

World Protocol Academy — Institute for Protocol, Diplomacy, Public Communication & Security Studies is a specialized academic and professional platform for knowledge development, training, research and measurable standards in protocol, diplomacy, public communication, security and institutional presence.

WPA does not present itself as a university degree-awarding institution. WPA is positioned as an independent academy and institute for professional excellence, research papers, applied methodology and certification programmes.

Five pillars of WPA Institute

1. Protocol Studies

State, diplomatic, institutional and ceremonial protocol.

2. Diplomacy & Security

Diplomatic practice, security coordination and crisis preparedness.

3. Public Communication

Public presence, institutional messaging, media discipline and public relations.

4. Professional Certification

Measurable professional tracks, certificates and practical competencies.

5. Research & Benchmarking

WPA working papers, policy briefs, case studies and the Global Protocol & Diplomacy Benchmark.

Institutional position: Negotiation is optional. Protocol is absolute. WPA develops this doctrine as a measurable, applicable and internationally understandable framework.
